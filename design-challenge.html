Design Challenge

We began by determining the design challenge, which is a worksheet from the Human Centered Design Toolkit that helps us form our final design question .

What is the problem you're trying to solve?

Letting people in hospice, who don’t have loved ones around anymore, know that they are loved through the gesture of giving flowers.

Frame it as a design question.

How might we reach more volunteers and donors through the website?

Now, state the ultimate impact you’re trying to have.

Give people in hospice flowers to show that they are loved and appreciated.

What are some possible solutions to your problem?

Hierarchy, high quality images, organization, fix icons that don’t make sense, fix busy pages, and add more white space

Finally, write down some of the context and constraints that you’re facing.

Organization will not interview with me because they are too busy. May have trouble collecting all content I need because they are in Portland.

Does your original question need a tweak? Try it again.

How might we organize the website to where volunteers and donors can easily be engaged with the bloom project?
